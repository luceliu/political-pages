Visualizing Facebook Posts from 9 U.S. Political Pages

By Luce Liu, Sherry Xie, Amy Zhu

We're three UBC students who are interested in exploring patterns concerning truthfulness and user engagement amongst political Facebook posts. The Facebooks posts explored here are sourced from nine American political pages over the course of a week in September 2016. Six of the pages are hyperpartisan, and three are considered mainstream.

The truthfulness ratings were provided by Buzzfeed News, as part of their analysis on the topic which you can read here. You can explore the dataset here.
